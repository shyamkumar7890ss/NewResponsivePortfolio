Deploy with Netlify in 5 Minutes

Deploying your website with Netlify is simple:
1) Push your code to GitHub.
2) Sign in to Netlify.
3) Click 'New Site from Git'.
4) Connect your GitHub repo.
5) Click deploy. Netlify auto-builds and hosts your site with a live URL.
